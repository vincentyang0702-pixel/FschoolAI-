# NeuroGlass + Blockchain: China Manufacturing Restrictions & Solutions

## The Critical Question

**You're asking:** "NeuroGlass stores data on-device via blockchain. Crypto wallets are illegal to manufacture in China. Does this affect us?"

**Short answer:** YES, it affects how you classify and manufacture, but NO, it doesn't prevent manufacturing in China. Here's why and how to solve it.

---

## China's Crypto Restrictions

### What's Illegal in China

```
Illegal to manufacture in China:
├─ Hardware crypto wallets (Ledger, Trezor)
├─ Devices designed primarily for crypto
├─ Devices marketed as "blockchain storage"
├─ Devices with built-in crypto exchange
└─ Devices that facilitate crypto trading

Why:
├─ China banned crypto trading (2017)
├─ China banned ICOs (2017)
├─ China banned crypto mining (2021)
├─ China wants to control financial systems
└─ Crypto hardware is seen as circumventing control
```

### What's Legal in China

```
Legal to manufacture in China:
├─ General-purpose computing devices
├─ Audio recorders
├─ Data storage devices
├─ Smart cards
├─ Devices that HAPPEN to use blockchain
├─ Devices NOT marketed as crypto
└─ Devices marketed for other primary purposes
```
