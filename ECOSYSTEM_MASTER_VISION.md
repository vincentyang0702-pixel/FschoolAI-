## Architecture Principles (For the Engineering Team)

1. **One Brain, One Identity** — The same NeuroAGI brain UUID identifies the student across every layer: academic, financial, career, social. One ID for everything.

2. **Brain is the Source of Truth** — Every product writes signals to the brain. The brain is not a sync target — it is the origin. FschoolAI DB, Study Rooms, Venture Studio, Shadow Economy — all write to the brain.

3. **MCP + A2A Protocol** — Every product connects to the brain via MCP (Model Context Protocol). Every agent talks to other agents via A2A (Agent-to-Agent Protocol). No custom sync jobs. No brittle pipelines.

4. **Data Minimization for Partners** — Partners and institutions never see individual data. They see aggregate signals only. Student owns their brain. Always.

5. **Token Economy is Auditable** — Every FST token earn and spend is logged with timestamp, source, and amount. Required for financial compliance in fintech layer.

6. **Student Verification is Cryptographic** — University email + Canvas OAuth = proof of student status. This is what enables financial products and the global credential layer.
