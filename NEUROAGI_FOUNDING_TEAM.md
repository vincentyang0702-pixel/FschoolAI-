## Page 1 — The Problem

Every human being alive today has a brain that is completely disconnected from their digital and physical world.

You use your phone. You use your laptop. You sit in lectures, attend meetings, read papers, have conversations that change how you think. Every one of these experiences generates cognitive signal — new knowledge, new patterns, new understanding. And every one of them disappears the moment you close the tab, leave the room, or fall asleep.

The result is a catastrophic inefficiency in human cognition. The average knowledge worker spends 20% of their working week re-finding information they have already encountered. Students re-learn material they have already studied because no system tracked what they actually understood versus what they merely read. Researchers duplicate literature reviews because there is no persistent model of what they already know. Every AI tool available today — GPT-4, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity — resets when you close the window. Day 1 and Day 1,000 are identical. The model does not know you. It cannot predict you. It has no stake in whether you actually learn anything.

This is not a software problem. It is an architectural problem. Every AI system ever built has been designed to be universal — to serve every human identically. The assumption is that intelligence is general. The reality is that intelligence is always personal. A model that knows everything about the world but nothing about you specifically is not intelligent about you. It is a very fast library.

The disconnection between the human brain and its digital and physical environment is the bottleneck on human potential. It is not a niche EdTech problem. It is a civilizational problem. The person who closes this gap will have built the most important infrastructure of the 21st century.

**NeuroAGI is closing this gap.**

## Page 2 — The Architecture

### The Nested Intelligence Stack

The AI field has a hierarchy problem that almost nobody talks about clearly.

At the top sits the **Universal Model** — GPT-4, Claude, Gemini. These systems approximate a model of all human knowledge. They are extraordinary at reasoning over language. They are completely blind to the specific human in front of them.

Below that sits the **World Model** — a causal, physical model of how reality works. Yann LeCun has argued for a decade that AI cannot be truly intelligent without one. The Sapient Company is working on the perceptual layer of this problem. It remains an open research question. No one has solved it.

Below that sits the **Personal World Model** — a model of how *your specific world* works. Your environment. Your relationships. Your physical context. Your behavioral patterns. No one is building this. This is NeuroAGI's primary research and engineering problem.

Below that sits the **Personal Brain Model** — a model of how *your specific mind* works. Your cognitive architecture. Your knowledge graph. Your learning patterns. Your memory consolidation. Your procrastination signals. Your mastery trajectory. This is the layer that makes the personal world model actionable.

```
Universal Model     →  GPT-4, Claude (exists — we use it)
World Model         →  LeCun/JEPA, Sapient (unsolved — we approximate)
Personal World Model →  Physical + digital context fusion (NeuroAGI builds this)
Personal Brain Model →  Cognitive architecture + knowledge graph (NeuroAGI builds this)
Sovereign Identity  →  DID + PBDS — the user owns all of it (NeuroAGI's moat)
```

NeuroAGI does not compete with OpenAI at the Universal Model layer. We use Claude as the reasoning engine. Our contribution is the personal layers — the layers that transform a universal model into a model that is genuinely intelligent about one specific human.

### The Signal Architecture

The personal world model is built from two classes of signal:

**Digital signals** — captured by FschoolAI (our first application): Canvas grades, assignment submissions, study session duration, knowledge gap detection, writing evolution, procrastination patterns, professor feedback analysis. These signals build the cognitive layer of the brain.

**Physical signals** — captured by the Neural Card (our hardware layer): lecture audio processed on-device, ambient environment context, movement and posture patterns, focus state inference. These signals build the physical world layer of the brain. Raw audio never leaves the device. Only abstracted signals — duration, topic, context, confidence — are written to the brain. This is privacy by architecture, not by policy.

The fusion of both signal classes produces something neither can produce alone: a model of the person in their world, not just their digital behavior or their physical environment in isolation.

### The Sovereignty Layer

Every other context platform owns the data it collects. OpenAI owns your conversation history. Google owns your search. Canvas owns your grades. When you stop paying, the data disappears or becomes inaccessible.

NeuroAGI's architecture is built on three Web5 primitives that make the brain genuinely sovereign:

**Decentralized Identity (DID):** Each user's brain is anchored to a cryptographic key they hold. The DID is a W3C standard — production-ready today. If NeuroAGI shuts down, the identity persists. The brain is not tied to an account. It is tied to a key.

**Personal Brain Data Store (PBDS):** The knowledge graph, signals, and cognitive patterns are stored in a format the user can export, migrate, and revoke access to. In v1 this is a Supabase database scoped to the user's DID. In v3 it migrates to user-controlled decentralized storage. The architecture is designed for this migration from day one.

**Verifiable Brain Credentials (VBC):** Learning milestones, mastery levels, and cognitive profiles become cryptographically signed credentials. An employer can verify that a candidate demonstrated statistical reasoning at the 85th percentile over three years without ever seeing the raw brain data. This is the resume of the 21st century.

The honest caveat: full decentralization is 2–3 years away. v1 uses Supabase with a DID layer on top. The positioning is architecturally correct even if the full technical reality is not yet complete.

## Page 3 — What We Have Built and Where We Are Going

### FschoolAI — Proof of Concept

FschoolAI is the first application in the NeuroAGI ecosystem. It is a student second brain — a multi-agent AI system that connects to a student's Canvas LMS, builds a persistent cognitive model of their academic life, and intervenes proactively when it detects that the student is about to fall behind.

The product is live. It has real users. It has real brain data. It has a Chrome extension that syncs Canvas in real time, a multi-agent system that runs nightly reflection while the student sleeps, a knowledge graph that compounds with every session, and a motivation engine that detects avoidance patterns and sends interventions before the student opens the app.

FschoolAI is not the vision. It is the proof that the vision is technically feasible. Every student who uses FschoolAI for a year generates a brain that no competitor can replicate, no institution can claim, and no algorithm can commoditize. That brain is the foundation of NeuroAGI.

### The Ecosystem Architecture

NeuroAGI is a platform company, not a product company. FschoolAI is the first-party application that proves the brain model works. The long-term architecture is an open ecosystem:

**The Brain OS** — the core platform. A sovereign personal world model that any AI agent can read from and write to, with user-controlled permissions. Agents get rich context on day one. Users never lose their data when they switch agents.

**The Agent Ecosystem** — any developer can build an agent that connects to the NeuroAGI brain. A fitness agent, a professional agent, a creative agent, a research agent — all reading from and writing to the same brain. The brain gets richer with every agent added. The user is never locked in.

**The Hardware Layer** — the Neural Card is the first physical sensor in the ecosystem. The long-term vision is a partnership network with Chinese AI hardware manufacturers — Midea, Haier, Xiaomi, and others who are embedding AI into every physical product. An AI air conditioner does not need to be smart. It needs to connect to the NeuroAGI brain and receive a signal: "this user's optimal study temperature is 19°C." The intelligence lives in the brain. The device is an actuator.

**The Sovereignty Marketplace** — users can optionally monetize their own brain data. Anonymized, permissioned learning patterns sold to research institutions, educational publishers, or employers. The user gets paid. NeuroAGI takes a platform fee. FST tokens are the currency of this marketplace.
