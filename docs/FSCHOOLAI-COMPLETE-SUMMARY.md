## PART 2: YOUR PDF - THE ARCHITECTURE

### What Your PDF Describes

Your PDF is a **complete learning OS architecture** with:

#### **The 6 Neural Strings (Data Capture)**

These are the 6 ways FschoolAI understands you:

1. **Behavioral Signal (为号)** - How you type, pause, delete, switch apps
   - What it tells: Anxiety, confusion, avoidance, flow state
   - Captured: 100% passive (background listener)

2. **Voice & Text Emotion (语绩)** - How you talk to FschoolAI
   - What it tells: Confidence, where you're stuck, what you're afraid of
   - Captured: Every conversation

3. **Historical Pattern (历数)** - Everything over time
   - What it tells: Your unique learning fingerprint
   - Captured: Automatic, gets more accurate every day

4. **Task Context (务)** - Course name, assignment, deadline, grade weight
   - What it tells: Stakes, time pressure, what help you need
   - Captured: Canvas LMS integration (auto-pulled)

5. **Intent Signal (图号)** - What you want but haven't said
   - What it tells: Real goal vs stated goal
   - Captured: One question per session ("What do you want to finish today?")

6. **Environment Signal (环号)** - Time of day, device, noise level, location
   - What it tells: 5am in bed is different from 3pm in library
   - Captured: Automatic (timestamp, device, ambient mic)

**PLUS:**

7. **Autonomous Content Ingestion (内摄)** - Everything you consume
   - Lectures, slides, PDFs, YouTube, Instagram, podcasts, articles, TikToks
   - Ingested, processed, integrated into your knowledge graph
   - Captured: Whisper API, Vision API, browser extension, OAuth

8. **Metacognitive Signal (认号)** - When you become aware of your own thinking
   - Tip-of-tongue moments, vanishing ideas, pre-verbal breakthroughs
   - Most valuable signal: Your thinking BEFORE your brain edits it
   - Captured: Behavioral micro-patterns (sudden stops, mid-sentence deletions, long pauses)

## PART 4: THE DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

### Why the Previous Design Didn't Work

The previous design was:
- ❌ Generic (looked like every other app)
- ❌ Centered (boring layouts)
- ❌ Flat (no depth, no personality)
- ❌ Didn't reflect the brand (FschoolAI is revolutionary, design should show it)

### The New Design Philosophy

**FschoolAI is not a tool. It's a second brain. It's alive. It understands you.**

The design should reflect this:

#### **1. Adaptive UI (The Brain Principle)**
- UI changes based on student's emotional state
- Anxious → Simple, supportive, step-by-step
- Focused → Complex, challenging, deep
- Exhausted → Minimal, do-more-ask-less
- Confident → Expansive, exploratory, creative

#### **2. Emotional Design (The Human Principle)**
- Colors reflect emotional states (not just brand colors)
- Typography shows understanding (not generic)
- Spacing shows breathing room (not cramped)
- Micro-interactions show the system is alive (not static)

#### **3. Asymmetric Layout (The Unique Principle)**
- Not centered, not grid-based
- Reflects how the brain works (non-linear, connected)
- Assignment on left, brain insights on right
- Knowledge graph visible (showing connections)
- Emotional state visible (showing understanding)

#### **4. Depth & Texture (The Sophisticated Principle)**
- Subtle shadows (shows depth)
- Gentle gradients (shows sophistication)
- Blur effects (shows focus)
- Grain/noise (shows humanity)

#### **5. Interactive Nuances (The Alive Principle)**
- Fluid transitions (not snappy)
- Hover effects (show responsiveness)
- Entrance animations (show anticipation)
- Loading states (show thinking)

#### **6. Comprehensive Branding (The Consistent Principle)**
- Every element reinforces the brand
- Title: "FschoolAI - Your Second Brain"
- Description: "Understands you. Adapts to you. Learns for you."
- Logo: Brain + Student + Connection (integrated)
- Color: Warm, human, approachable (not corporate)
